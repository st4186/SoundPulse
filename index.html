More than an app, a community

In a market dominated by international giants, SoundPulse was born to fill a gap: human connection. We are the first streaming platform developed in Mexico that combines high-definition audio with an integrated social review system.

We believe human opinions are worth more than opaque algorithms. Our mission is to transform passive listeners into active curators, supporting the local indie scene and offering fair prices for the Mexican purchasing power.

  • 100% Mexican Developed
  • Focus on users aged 18-35
  • Community over Algorithms

Our Essence

Mission

Transform the way Mexicans discover and consume music, combining high-quality streaming with an active community of music critics. Empower passive listeners to become active curators, while supporting the local indie scene with fair prices.

Vision

To be Mexico's leading music streaming platform by 2030, recognized for our vibrant community of music lovers and for discovering more than 10,000 independent Mexican artists, creating an ecosystem where human recommendations matter more than algorithms.

Values

  • Transparency: Fair prices with no hidden terms
